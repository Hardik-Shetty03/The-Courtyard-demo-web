## The Vision

Imagine walking into your club on a Monday morning. Every court booking from the weekend is already logged. Every rupee — from a ₹50 water bottle to a ₹4,999 Elite membership — is tracked and categorized in your financial ledger. Your receptionist handled 40+ walk-in customers over the weekend, and every single one of them is now a contact in your system, ready to receive promotions, membership offers, and tournament invitations. No paper registers. No missing cash. No "I forgot to write it down."

**That's what this platform delivers.**

---

## The Problem We Solve

Running a premium sports club today means juggling a dozen disconnected systems:

| Pain Point | What Happens Today |
|:---|:---|
| **Court Bookings** | Phone calls, WhatsApp messages, and a paper diary at the front desk. Double bookings happen. Cancellations get lost. |
| **Walk-in Customers** | They play, they pay cash, they leave. You have zero data on who they are, how often they visit, or how much they spend. |
| **Coaching & Academy** | Attendance is tracked on paper sheets. Parents ask for progress updates and coaches have nothing documented. |
| **Inventory & Amenities** | Racket rentals, water bottles, energy drinks — sold at the counter but never properly tracked. Stock goes missing. |
| **Revenue Tracking** | At the end of the month, you're piecing together numbers from cash registers, UPI screenshots, and memory. Court revenue gets mixed with shop revenue. |
| **Memberships** | Managed in spreadsheets. Expiry dates are missed. Discounts are applied inconsistently. |

**The result?** Lost revenue, unhappy customers, zero data for business decisions, and a club that runs on people's memory instead of a system.

## What The Platform Delivers

### 🏟️ Smart Court Booking System

Your customers can book courts directly from their phones — anytime, anywhere. They pick a court, select their time slots, and pay instantly. A **QR code pass** is generated on their phone, which your receptionist simply scans when they walk in.

**What this means for you:**
- **No more double bookings.** The system blocks slots the instant they're reserved.
- **Peak-hour premium pricing.** Morning rush (6–9 AM) and evening prime time (5–10 PM) automatically charge higher rates. Off-peak hours charge the base rate. You set both prices per court.
- **24/7 booking.** Customers book at midnight for tomorrow morning. No phone calls needed.
- **Cancellation tracking.** Every cancelled booking is logged with timestamps.

---

### 💳 Spot POS Billing at the Reception Desk

Your receptionist gets a powerful checkout screen. When a walk-in customer arrives or a player wants to grab a drink and rent a racket, the process is simple:

1. **Select the customer** from the list (or enter a new guest's email).
2. **Add items to the cart** — court slots, racket rentals, water bottles, towels, anything.
3. **Choose payment method** — Cash, Card, or charge to the player's prepaid Club Wallet.
4. **Done.** Receipt generated. Inventory updated. Ledger entry created.

**The key innovation:** Every walk-in guest must provide their **email address**. Even if they never sign up for an account, their purchase history is stored. The moment they decide to register on your website or app, all their past visits, purchases, and spending data automatically appear in their profile. **You turn anonymous walk-ins into identifiable, reachable customers — every single time.**

---

### 📦 Inventory Management

No more guessing how many rackets are available for rental or whether the fridge is stocked.

- **Real-time stock tracking.** Every item sold through the POS counter automatically reduces the stock count.
- **Low stock visibility.** Your admin dashboard shows current stock levels at a glance.
- **Full control.** Add new items, adjust pricing, update quantities, or deactivate items that are temporarily unavailable.
- **Items include:** Racket Rentals, Premium Water, Energy Drinks, Towel Service, Guest Pass Fees, Trainer Fees — and any custom items you add.

---

### 🏅 Membership Tiers & Loyalty

Turn casual players into committed members with a tiered membership system:

| Tier | Monthly Price | Court Discount | Coaching Discount | Special Perks |
|:---|:---|:---|:---|:---|
| **Basic** | ₹999 | 10% off all courts | — | Priority booking alerts |
| **Pro** | ₹1,999 | 25% off all courts | 10% off coaching | Access to member events |
| **Elite** | ₹4,999 | **100% free courts** | 20% off coaching | VIP lounge, all events free |

**What this means for you:**
- **Predictable recurring revenue.** Monthly membership fees create a stable income baseline.
- **Higher spending per member.** Members who get free courts still spend on coaching, amenities, and tournaments.
- **Automatic discount application.** No manual calculations at the desk — the system applies the correct discount based on membership tier, every time.

---

### 🎾 Coaching Academy & Progress Tracking

Your coaching program becomes a structured, trackable academy:

- **Course Management.** Create multi-week coaching programs with defined schedules, capacity limits, and pricing. Example: *"Beginner Bootcamp — 10 Days — Mon/Wed/Fri 5:00–6:30 PM — ₹8,000"*
- **Enrollment & Capacity Control.** Set a maximum class size. The system stops accepting enrollments once it's full.
- **QR-Based Attendance.** Every enrolled student gets a unique QR pass. Scanned daily at check-in. Attendance is logged automatically.
- **Skill Progress Reports.** Coaches rate students across five skill areas (Footwork, Serve, Dinking, Backhand, Stamina) on a 1–5 scale after each session. Parents and players can see their improvement over time.
- **Schedule Change Notifications.** If a course schedule changes, every enrolled student is automatically notified.

**What this means for you:**
- **Coaching becomes a premium, scalable product** — not just informal one-on-one sessions.
- **Parents see tangible progress** — which drives renewals and word-of-mouth referrals.
- **Coach accountability improves** — attendance and skill evaluations are documented, not verbal.

---

### 🏆 Tournament Management

Launch and manage competitive events directly from the platform:

- **Create tournaments** with details like prize pool, entry fee, date, and promotional graphics.
- **Online registration.** Players register with one click. You see the participant count in real time.
- **Broadcast announcements.** Push tournament promotions to every user in the system instantly.
- **Track winners and results** after the event concludes.

**What this means for you:** Tournaments drive footfall, create buzz, and attract new customers. Having a digital system means fewer no-shows, better planning, and a professional image.

---

### 💰 Club Wallet & Tab System

Give your regular players the convenience of a **prepaid Club Wallet:**

- Players load money into their wallet (online via card, or at the desk via cash/card).
- All purchases — court bookings, amenities, coaching — can be paid instantly from the wallet.
- **Split payments** are supported: part wallet, part cash/card.
- **Postpaid "Tab" option:** Trusted regulars can accumulate charges on a tab and settle later — via wallet, card, or cash at the desk.

**What this means for you:**
- **Money stays in your ecosystem.** Wallet balances are pre-committed revenue.
- **Faster transactions at the desk.** No fumbling with change or waiting for UPI confirmations.
- **Reduced cash handling risks.** Digital wallet payments are fully audited.

### 📊 The Owner's Dashboard — Complete Financial Visibility

This is where the real power sits. Your admin dashboard gives you a bird's-eye view of the entire business:

#### Revenue at a Glance
- **Total Revenue** across all streams — Courts, Coaching, Memberships, and Amenities.
- **Revenue broken down by category** — so you know exactly how much courts earn vs. coaching vs. the pro shop.

#### Court Utilization
- **Percentage of slots booked** across all courts over a rolling 7-day window.
- **Peak hours heatmap** — which time slots are the most popular, helping you make pricing and staffing decisions.

#### Membership Growth
- **Active member counts** broken down by tier (Basic / Pro / Elite).

#### Coaching Analytics
- **Most booked coach** — identify your star performers.
- **Course capacity rates** — are your courses filling up, or do you need better marketing?
- **Average attendance rates** — track whether enrolled students are actually showing up.

#### The Master Ledger
A single, unified financial log that shows **every transaction** that has ever happened in your club:
- Court bookings (online and spot)
- Coaching session payments
- Academy enrollments
- Membership purchases
- Amenity sales (separated from court revenue)
- Wallet top-ups and deductions
- Tab settlements
- Admin refunds and overrides

Every entry shows **who paid, how much, when, what for, and how they paid** (cash, card, wallet, tab, or split). No more end-of-month guesswork.

### 🔔 Automated Notifications

The system communicates with your customers automatically:

- **Booking confirmations** — instant notification when a court is reserved.
- **Pre-game reminders** — "Your court session starts in 1 hour. Arrive 10 minutes early!"
- **Membership activation alerts** — congratulatory message when a tier is upgraded.
- **Course schedule updates** — if a class time changes, every student is notified immediately.
- **Tournament announcements** — broadcast to the entire user base.
- **Check-in confirmations** — "Welcome to The Courtyard! Your check-in has been verified."

**What this means for you:** Fewer no-shows, better communication, and a professional experience that makes customers feel valued.

---

### 👥 Staff Access Control

Not everyone needs to see everything. The platform has built-in role-based access:

| Role | What They Can Do | What They Cannot Do |
|:---|:---|:---|
| **Owner / Admin** | Full access — analytics, pricing, ledger, user management, all operations | — |
| **Receptionist** | Scan QR check-ins, create spot bookings, manage enrollments, top-up wallets | View revenue figures, modify pricing, delete users, access the master ledger |
| **Coach** | View assigned students, record attendance, submit skill evaluations | Access financials, modify bookings, manage inventory |
| **Player / Member** | Book courts, enroll in courses, view personal dashboard & ledger, manage wallet | Access admin tools |

**What this means for you:** Your receptionist handles the desk efficiently without seeing your revenue numbers. Coaches focus on training without accessing business data. You maintain full control.
